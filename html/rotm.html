Buffalo chicken burger

Ingredients

  • 8 boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 100ml buffalo hot sauce
  • 2 tsp sweet smoked paprika
  • 3 tbsp maple syrup
  • 1 tbsp white wine vinegar
  • 1Â½ tsp garlic granules
  • 30g butter, melted and cooled
  • 4 slices monterey jack cheese, cut in half
  • 4 brioche burger buns, split

Method

  • STEP 1: In a large bowl, mix hot sauce, paprika, maple syrup, vinegar, garlic granules, and butter. Pour over chicken, toss, cover, and chill overnight.
  • STEP 2: The next day, prepare the ranch slaw. Combine cabbage, onion, celery, mayonnaise, sour cream, vinegar, and chives in a medium bowl. Season to taste and mix until well-combined.
  • STEP 3: Light the barbecue. Grill the chicken for 8 minutes on each side, basting with leftover marinade. Alternatively, cook the chicken under a hot grill for 8-10 minutes on each side, basting with marinade. Add cheese slices and melt for 1 minute. Place chicken thighs on bun bottoms, top with slaw, and sandwich with bun tops.
